Dodgeball Team

Introducing Team Sonic! a dodgeball team made entirely of heroes (and a rival or two). With speed, precision, and teamwork, no one can outplay this crew.

1. Sonic the Hedgehog

The fastest thing alive! Sonic can dodge any throw and launch a return shot before the ball even lands.

2. Tails (Miles Prower)

Using his twin tails to hover, Tails can dodge from above and provide perfect mid-air passes to teammates.

3. Knuckles the Echidna

Built for power! Knuckles can throw dodgeballs with incredible force and block almost anything coming his way.

4. Amy Rose

Energetic, quick-thinking, and fearless. Amy’s accuracy and enthusiasm keep morale high and pressure on the opponents.

5. Shadow the Hedgehog

Cool, calculated, and incredibly fast. Shadow’s chaos control lets him teleport around the field for perfect plays.
