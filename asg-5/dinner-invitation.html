You're Invited to a Dinner Party!

Guests:

Carlos Oliveira

Carlos is my favorite character from the Resident Evil game series, so i would want him to attend my dinner party. In resident evil 3, Carlos is a mercernary hired by the Umberella Corporation and works under the Umberella Biohazard Countermeasure Service. I enjoy his personality and I think he'd be the life of the party, making jokes and being a fun person to be around.

Jill Valentine

Jill is the protagnist in Resident evil 3. and an aquaintance to Carlos. She works for RPD'S S.T.A.R.S. unit before leaving to investiagte the shady business of the Umberella Co. I'm inviting her because I feel like she can't be included without Carlos. She would have fun in her own way while being responsible.I admire her strong personality, humor, and wit.

Rebecca Chambers

Rebecca is a character that is seriously underrated, yet extremely important to the plot pre-resident evil 1. Appearing in Resident Evil 0, Rebecca is a medic on the S.T.A.R.S. Bravo Team investigating murders, and eventually leading her to a train where she encounters the beginning of the zombie outbreak. I chose to invite her because she holds a specialplace in my heart. Despite appearing in one game, her pesonality, smarts, and attitude made her instantly memorable to me.
